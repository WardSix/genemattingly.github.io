About Gene

Gene Mattingly is a Texas-based photographer with a passion for capturing the energy of events and the authenticity of portraits. His cinematic approach blends dramatic lighting with candid storytelling, creating images that feel both timeless and intensely present.

Whether documenting life’s celebrations or crafting intimate portraits, Gene delivers a bespoke experience that prioritizes connection, comfort, and artistry.
