About Gene

Portraits & events with cinematic care.

Every session starts with conversations about the people, the energy, and the memories you want to freeze in time. I mix editorial lighting with gently guided moments so families, seniors, and milestone crews feel grounded, seen, and free to let genuine emotion surface.

Based in Schertz, I regularly photograph in San Antonio, New Braunfels, Boerne, Cibolo, and the surrounding Hill Country—scouting rooftops, creekside parks, and modern venues that fit the story we’re telling.

Portrait offerings include seniors, individuals, and creative teams with multi-look styling, retouching, and optional reels, while event coverage for reunions, milestones, and celebrations balances candid storytelling with hero frames.
