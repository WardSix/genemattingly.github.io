About Gene

Gene Mattingly is a Texas-based photographer and videographer with a passion for capturing the energy of events and the authenticity of portraits. His cinematic approach blends dramatic lighting with candid storytelling, creating imagery and films that feel both timeless and intensely present.

Whether documenting life’s celebrations or crafting intimate portraits, Gene delivers a bespoke experience that prioritizes connection, comfort, and artistry.
